CAPSTONE POSTER SERIES

About the Project

Seniors are required to create work that showcases an acquired understanding of design and art accumulated during their studies.


What I did

Focusing on a illustrative style and minimal composition I chose to create a poster series paying homage to some influential musicians who met their ends in an untimely manner. These were printed digitally on French cream speckletone paper. They can also be seen displayed on the Drury Visual Communication

site.
